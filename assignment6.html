Activity 3
I found this activity very relevant as I am currently looking for work. I had trouble writing a resume and needed the help of a family friend who works as a headhunter to get my resume into shape. I think the resources provided in this tutorial largely mirror her advice and help to develop a solid resume. I thought the concept of not only defining your skills but explaining them and relating how they are relevant to the job you are applying for was especially important. Tailoring your resume to the job you are applying for is very effective. For example, I come from grocery retail management. If I was looking for a retail job I would go into detail about the tasks I performed, my responsibilities, and customer service skills. If I was looking for an IT job I could go into detail about how I helped launch tech resets and train new stores on using new systems while including more detail about my education and certifications. The last point of listing accomplishments over responsibilities also stuck out to me. Saying “I helped to successfully open or re-open a dozen locations” sounds a lot better than saying one of your responsibilities was helping at other stores.
